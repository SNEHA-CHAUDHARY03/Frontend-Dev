The Art of Typography

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed.

The evolution of typography spans centuries, from the invention of movable type to modern digital fonts. Good typography enhances readability and creates visual hierarchy.

Typography is the craft of endowing human language with a durable visual form.

Modern web typography offers incredible flexibility and control over how text is displayed. With CSS, we can adjust:

  • Font families and weights
  • Letter spacing and line height
  • Text alignment and decoration
  • And much more!
